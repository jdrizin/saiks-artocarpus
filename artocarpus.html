Maples (Acer) of Michigan: a Leaf Key

Dataset originally published in IDENT (Morse 1969) format, 13 March 1969 by L.E. Morse, then transferred to DELTA (Dallwitz 1971) format for use in ONLINE (Pankhurst 1986) 26 February 1987 by G.F. Guala, then transferred to SLIKS (Guala 2004) format 11 July 2005 by G.F. Guala. Then scribbled on for test purposes by G.A. Alexander (2006).

SAIKS is based on SLIKS by Stinger. SAIKS is copyright (c) 2006 Greg Alexander, to be distributed under the GPLv2.
